Take Off Mondays - Your Day

TakeOffMondays is a non-profit foundation aimed at helping people make time to work on things they care about. Take off Mondays is about taking off a Monday and making it yours. Making time so you can work on things that your care about! Most of us spend the week working on things just to get by.
... Paint a picture, write a story, start a non-profit, learn to fly ...
You know what it is like on Sunday. Wouldn't Sunday be so much better and enjoyable if you knew you were not going to work, but you were going to work on something you cared about.
